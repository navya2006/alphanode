SPACEFFIC

Traffic Classifier

ABOUT SPACEFFIC

Spaceffic is a universal space traffic control service that detects any object/spacecraft in the vicinity of another spacecraft and guides it to take appropriate action (stop/wait/go) through a traffic light installed at regular intervals on all authorized space travel routes.

OUR AIM

We aim to make space travel safer and more efficient for all. As of now, Spaceffic guides 180,000+ spacecrafts, from 1,200+ rocket launch bases, flying to or from all the planets of the solar system.

WORKING

We have installed cameras, processing units and motion trackers on the traffic signals which capture videos that are broken down into frames. This will be used to calculate the traffic density on that particular route and then accordingly manipulate the traffic signal on the basis of the instructions generated through the AI model.

THE PROBLEM

With the colonisation of all planets in the solar system and commercialisation of space travel, lakhs of spacecrafts are travelling at the same time in space. Moreover, 27,000+ pieces of orbital debris, or “space junk,” has been tracked. Much more debris, too small to be tracked, but large enough to threaten human spaceflight and robotic missions, exists in the near Earth space environment.

To regulate their movement, and to avoid collisions between orbital debris/meteoroids or asteroids, spaceffic has come up with a simple yet effective solution.

OUR SOLUTION

The concept of a traffic light on roads has been utilised with various alterations making it suitable for use in space. We have made a self-sufficient traffic system infused with AI, Deep ML which will be integrated with a processing unit and motion tracker fitted on each traffic light with an external connection to a camera.

The traffic lights would alter their duration on the basis of a pre-fed algorithm in the processing unit module which in turn will optimize the availability of routes and reduce congestion. The traffic light will be solar powered and thus will be in working condition 24/7. We would also be adding batteries for a backup.
